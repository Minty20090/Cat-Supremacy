Meet the Cats!

Mimi

This is Mimi! We rescued her from a steel workshop almost 12 years ago. A few workers were feeding Mimi and her newborn litter. We don't know how old she is but we know she's the oldest of our cats. She's now living her best life with lots of cuddles and all the food she can eat.

Raisin

This is Raisin! She's Mimi's daughter, whom we rescued along with her mother. Five-year-old me chose to name her Raisin since she looks sort of like a Raisin! She's probably the clingiest cat in our family. She loves taking naps with us. She will not let you sleep unless she's in the same bed as you.

Oreo

This is Oreo! She's also Mimi's daughter. She looks sort of like an oreo because of the white ring of fur around her neck. She's the loudest of all of our cats. She has an unhealthy habit of going up to your ear and meowing as loud as she can.

Boba

This is Boba! He's our newest cat. We rescued him two years ago after he got injured in cat fight. He's the cuddliest of the cats. He's the only one that'll climb onto your lap. Unfortunately, he's also the heaviest. This means that in summer, it's like being smothered by a giant mass of fur.
